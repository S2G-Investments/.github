# S2G Investments Engineering

We build internal tools, data pipelines, and AI-assisted applications that help
the S2G team find, evaluate, and support mission-driven companies.

## What we work on

- **Internal applications** for portfolio, pipeline, and operations workflows
- **Data pipelines & integrations** that connect our core business systems
- **AI-assisted tooling** for research, document processing, and team productivity

## How we build

We favor modern, cloud-native, secure-by-default architectures: single sign-on,
least-privilege access, and short-lived credentials. The large majority of our
work lives in private repositories.
